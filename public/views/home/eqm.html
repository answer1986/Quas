Quality

SoftExpert EQM provides the means to implement and simplify closed-loop quality and compliance programs through joining together all key quality elements, including processes, documents, performance indicators, NCRs, CAPAs, audits, training records, competency assessments, inspection plans, SPC charts, and many others. By improving operational efficiencies in all quality-related processes, the software reduces risk and lowers cost associated with regulatory compliance.
